BYU Law School Information

General Information for Law School

  1. Preparation
    • You need to perform well in your undergraduate studies to get in to BYU Law. The average LSAT and GPA is 168 and 3.92 respectivly
    • Since law school is a heavy investment you need to make sure that's what you want to do, so we recomend you go and study about the day to day life of a lawyer and even get an intenship working with one.
  2. Admittance - BYU Law takes several things into consideration when looking at your application:
    • You'll need a polished resume showing how you've excelled in your studies and experiences
    • You'll need to submit a one page write-up about yourself
    • You'll also need some recomendations from teachers
  3. Career
    • After 1L you'll need to find what area of law interests you and begin taking those classes and then apply for internships in that field
    • You will then need to begin networking and taking into consideration where you want to live and what you want to make
